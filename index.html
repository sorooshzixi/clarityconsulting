About Our Practice

Founded in 2018 by four UCLA engineering graduates, Clarity Engineering brings together over 30 years of combined experience in structural design and analysis.

Based in Santa Monica, we serve clients throughout California with innovative, cost-effective structural solutions that prioritize safety, sustainability, and architectural vision.

Our Approach

  • Collaborative process with architects and builders
  • State-of-the-art analysis tools and techniques
  • Meticulous attention to California building codes
  • Sustainable and resilient design principles

Soft-Story Ordinance Compliance

We specialize in helping property owners comply with soft-story retrofit ordinances throughout California, ensuring buildings meet seismic safety standards.

Our Soft-Story Services:

  • Initial evaluation and feasibility studies
  • Complete structural analysis and design
  • Permit-ready construction documents
  • Coordination with local building departments
  • Construction administration and inspection

Did You Know?

Many California cities have mandatory soft-story retrofit programs for vulnerable buildings. We can help determine if your property is affected.
